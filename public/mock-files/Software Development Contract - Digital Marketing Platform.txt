BACKGROUND - WHY WE'RE DOING THIS PROJECT

In Nigeria's rapidly growing digital economy, this agreement supports local innovation and business growth. The Client is a marketing agency helping Nigerian SMEs build their online presence, while the Developer is a Lagos-based software company specializing in African market solutions.

This project will create a comprehensive digital marketing automation platform tailored specifically for Nigerian businesses, including support for mobile money integration, local payment methods, and compliance with Nigerian data protection regulations.

1. PROJECT SCOPE - WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

1.1 The Developer will create a comprehensive digital marketing automation platform (the "Software") specifically designed for Nigerian SMEs, with the following features:

(a) Customer relationship management (CRM) with Nigerian business contact preferences
(b) Email and SMS marketing automation (including support for Nigerian telecom networks)
(c) WhatsApp Business API integration for direct customer engagement
(d) Social media posting for local platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, TikTok)
(e) Lead scoring and nurturing workflows adapted for Nigerian business culture
(f) Analytics dashboard with Nigerian Naira currency support
(g) Integration with popular Nigerian payment gateways (Paystack, Flutterwave, Interswitch)
(h) Mobile-responsive design optimized for Nigeria's mobile-first internet usage
(i) Multi-tenant architecture supporting up to 500 Nigerian SMEs
(j) Offline capability for areas with unreliable internet connectivity

1.2 The Software will be developed using modern technologies:
   - Frontend: React.js with mobile-first responsive design
   - Backend: Node.js with Express framework
   - Database: PostgreSQL with MongoDB for unstructured data
   - Cloud hosting: AWS Africa (Cape Town) for optimal Nigerian performance

1.3 Special Nigerian features:
   - Support for Pidgin English in user interfaces
   - Integration with Nigerian business registration databases
   - Compliance with Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
   - Multi-currency support (Naira, Dollar, Pounds)
   - Integration with Nigerian tax authorities for automated compliance

2. PROJECT TIMELINE - WHEN WE'LL DELIVER

2.1 Project milestones and delivery schedule:
- Project kickoff and requirements gathering: November 15, 2024
- UI/UX design with Nigerian user testing: December 15, 2024
- Core platform development (50% complete): February 1, 2025
- Alpha version with beta user testing: April 1, 2025
- Beta version with Nigerian SME pilot clients: May 15, 2025
- Final version delivery and staff training: June 30, 2025

2.2 Time is crucial for Nigerian market opportunities. The Developer will provide weekly progress reports and will notify the Client immediately of any potential delays.

3. PAYMENT TERMS - HOW MUCH AND WHEN

3.1 Total project fee: ₦45,000,000 (Forty-Five Million Naira)

3.2 Payment schedule:
- 30% (₦13,500,000) upon contract signing and project initiation
- 25% (₦11,250,000) upon completion of UI/UX design phase
- 25% (₦11,250,000) upon delivery of alpha version
- 20% (₦9,000,000) upon final delivery and acceptance

3.3 Payments will be made in Nigerian Naira to the Developer's GTBank account. Invoices are due within 14 days of receipt to support Nigerian cash flow requirements. Late payments will incur interest at 2% per month.
